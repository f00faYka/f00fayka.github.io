ProjectManager

I am a Project Manager at IT&T System Integrator AVENCOM LLP. I have passed the full road here: IT support - Field/System Engineer - Team Lead - Project Manager. Sometimes our projects are very challenging: "to design infrastructure for Vessel Traffic Management system and deploy it in Caspian Sea" and sometimes are not: "Install telecom equipment in the middle of Kazakhstan's nowhere". WebDeveloper

I am a Web Developer self educated junior. It has always been interesting to me to create something by my own and make others life better by this. During my studying in University 15 years ago I practiced my Delphi skill with different pet projects like: #Virtual Desktop App, #Tiny Process Killer, #Win XP Registry Tweaker, ShortCut Everywhere. Right now I am on junior level knowledge of the starting tools: #Python/Django, HTML5, CSS3, DOM JS. First step is to try to take works from freelance websites to build portfolio of real projects. Later I will start to learn JS frameworks: #React, #Angular, #Vue and may be find real development projects with professionals teams.
